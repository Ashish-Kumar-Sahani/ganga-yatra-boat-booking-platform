# 1. Vision

The GangaYatra Platform is not intended to remain only a boat booking platform.

The long-term objective is to become India's largest **Smart River Mobility Platform**, integrating transportation, tourism, government services, AI, IoT, analytics, and SaaS into a single ecosystem.

The platform should eventually support:

- River Transportation
- Tourism
- Ferry Services
- Cruise Booking
- Government Monitoring
- Smart City Integration
- AI Automation
- Enterprise Fleet Management
